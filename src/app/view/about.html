whatsPup

WhatsPup was created as our final project for the Front-End Engineering cohort at the Iron Yard in Orlando, FL. The idea for this project came from our desire as pet owner to receive real-time updates from pet-sitters with information about their visits with our pets. As a result of three intensive months of learning front-end development and two weeks of work, WhatsPup was built so that pet-sitters can easily communicate with their clients.

The technology required to build this application includes: The AngularJS framework, Firebase for data storage, Gulp for build automation, Mandrill for sending email, Bootstrap for styling, and Cloudinary to support image uploading.
